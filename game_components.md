# 💎 SHARDFALL — Game Components Reference

This document lists all the asymmetric powers, secret goals, and chaotic events in the Shardfall universe.

---

## 🧙 Seeker Powers (Characters)
Each player chooses one Seeker at the start of the game.

| Seeker | Element | Power | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fire Seeker** | 🔥 | `fire_extract_bonus` | When harvesting from a **Fire Portal**, you may pay **+1 stability** extra to gain **3 additional Gems** (massive haul). |
| **Water Seeker** | 💧 | `free_stabilize` | Once per round, you may **Stabilize** a Portal for **free** (0 Gems cost). |
| **Earth Seeker** | 🌿 | `trade_discount` | Your **Bank Trades** cost **2:1** instead of 3:1. |
| **Air Seeker** | ⚡ | `extra_action` | You have **3 actions** per turn instead of 2. *(Limit: Max 1 Harvest action per turn).* |
| **Void Seeker** | 🔮 | `build_discount` | All **Constructs** cost **1 fewer Gem** to build (removes the cheapest type). |
| **Wanderer** | 🧭 | `double_explore` | Gain **2 Explorer Tokens** (instead of 1) when you are the first to harvest a new Portal type. |
| **Sentinel** | 🛡️ | `collapse_bonus` | **Immune to backlash.** If a Portal collapses while you are harvesting, gain **2 random Gems** from the wreckage. |
| **Diplomat** | 🤝 | `toll_immune` | **Never pays Tolls** when harvesting from a Portal claimed by another player. |

## 📜 Contract Cards (Secret Goals)
Players start with 2 secret contracts. Completing them gives high VP; failing them loses -2 VP.

| Contract | Icon | VP | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Guardian** | 🛡️ | 6 | Have **5+ Guardian Tokens** at game end. |
| **Universal Explorer** | 🧭 | 8 | Discover all **5 Portal types** (Fire, Water, Earth, Air, Void). |
| **Essence Hoarder** | 💰 | 5 | End the game with exactly **10 Gems** in hand. |
| **Master Builder** | 🏗️ | 7 | Build at least **2 Large Constructs**. |
| **Toll Collector** | 🤝 | 5 | Collect **5+ Gems** in tolls from other players. |
| **Portal Warden** | 🌀 | 6 | Successfully **Stabilize 4+ different Portals**. |
| **Specialist** | 🎯 | 4 | End with **7+ Gems of a single type**. |
| **Rainbow** | 💎 | 7 | End with at least **2 of each Gem type**. |
| **Trader** | ♻️ | 5 | Complete **6+ trades** (Bank or Player trades). |
| **Fire Lord** | 🔥 | 4 | Harvest from **3+ different Fire Portal cards**. |
| **Water Lord** | 💧 | 4 | Harvest from **3+ different Water Portal cards**. |
| **Daredevil** | 💀 | 5 | Perform a **Deep Harvest 3+ times**. |
| **Fortress** | 🏰 | 6 | Build **3+ Constructs** that have abilities (not just VP). |
| **World Saver** | 🌍 | 8 | The **Fracture Track** is at 2 or less at game end. |
| **Speed Builder** | ⚡ | 3 | Be the **first player** to build a Large Construct. |

## ⚡ Anomaly Cards (Events)
These cards are shuffled into the Portal deck and resolve immediately when drawn.

| Anomaly | Icon | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dimensional Surge** | 🌋 | All active Portals lose **1 stability**! |
| **Gem Rain** | 🎁 | Every player draws **2 random Gems** from the bag! |
| **Reality Shift** | 🔄 | The Construct Display is cleared and **completely replaced** with new options. |
| **Portal Storm** | ⚡ | The Portal with the **lowest stability** collapses immediately! |
| **Dimensional Calm** | 💫 | All active Portals gain **+1 stability** (up to their max). |
| **Convergence** | 🤝 | Each player with 2+ gems contributes 2. **Fracture -1** per contributor. |
| **Market Surge** | 🎭 | Market size expands to **6 gems**! Each player takes 1 free gem from the bag. |
| **Portal Merge** | 🌀 | The lowest-stability Portal is destroyed. The 2nd lowest gains **+2 stability**. |

## 🏗️ Construct Tiers (Pacing)
*To build a **Medium**, you need **2 Small**. To build a **Large**, you need **2 Small + 1 Medium**.*

*   **Small (Cost 3-4):** Provide early-game utility (discounts, better gathering).
*   **Medium (Cost 5-6):** Provide engine-building powers (persistent claims, extra gems).
*   **Large (Cost 7-10):** Provide massive late-game Victory Points and powerful multipliers.
